This is a Penalty Calculator App

  • This App is Build to calculate the penalties on books that are borrowed.
  • This App is only Applicable for the laws of Paksitan and UAE.
  • Here the weekend days of Pakistan are Saturday and Sundays where of UAE are Saturday and Friday
  • Penalty is calculated on the base of business days after 10 of book issued.
  • Each late business day will be penalized for Rs. 50
